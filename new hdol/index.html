Tokenomics

The HODL HOG tokenomics are designed for long-term growth, sustainability, and rewards for our dedicated community. Here's how the supply is structured:

Total Supply

10,000,000,000 HOG tokens will be issued to kickstart the ecosystem. A significant portion will be allocated to the community and ecosystem development.

Token Distribution

  • 50% Community & Airdrops
  • 20% Liquidity Pool
  • 10% Staking Rewards
  • 10% Development Fund
  • 10% Marketing & Partnerships

Utility

HOG tokens serve multiple purposes including staking, rewarding long-term holders, participating in the HOG-verse, and burning to reduce supply. This ensures that the token continues to provide value as the community grows.

Roadmap

Here’s our detailed roadmap that outlines our planned steps for building HODL HOG and achieving our vision. We are committed to delivering each phase with passion and consistency.

  • Phase 1 - Launch and Community Building

    Launch the HOG token, build community on X (formerly Twitter), Telegram, and Discord. Focus on increasing visibility and engaging with early adopters. First listing on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like SushiSwap and QuickSwap.

  • Phase 2 - Staking and Rewards

    Implement staking mechanisms for HOG holders to earn rewards. Launch initial marketing push to grow the community. Establish partnerships with influencers and meme crypto communities.

  • Phase 3 - HOG-verse Development

    Launch the **HOG-verse**, an interactive ecosystem for HOG holders, with unique features like NFTs, in-game rewards, and community engagement activities. Introduce DAO governance for decentralized decision-making.

  • Phase 4 - Expansion & Cross-Chain Partnerships

    Expand to other blockchain networks (e.g., Binance Smart Chain, Ethereum). Partner with other meme coins and major crypto projects for ecosystem growth. Host community events and burn events to keep supply low and demand high.
